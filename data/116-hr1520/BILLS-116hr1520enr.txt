H. R. 1520
Further Extension of Continuing Appropriations Act, 2021

That the Continuing Appropriations Act, 2021 (division A of Public Law 116–159) is further amended by striking the date specified in section 106(3) and inserting ''December 28, 2020''. 
This Act may be cited as the ''Further Extension of Continuing Appropriations Act, 2021''.
